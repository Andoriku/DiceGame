Welcome to Karate Tournament 2017

This is a dice based console game designed to pit you against many oponents in a Grand Tournament format.

The rules:

  1. On your attack turn you can pick one of 4 moves to attack with Super Punch (sp), Flaming Kick (fk), Thunder Chop (tc), or Barbaric Throw (bt).
  2. On your defense turn you can pick between High Block (hb) and Low Guard (lg).
  3. You and your opponent will trade blows until a victor emerges! if you want to quit out early, enter "im done" and you will exit the program.

Now go fufill your destiny, fighter!
